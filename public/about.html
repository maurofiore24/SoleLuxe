✦ SECTION I

Sovereignty Above Corporate Convenience

At SoleLuxe, we believe that your digital presence and creative brand is your absolute, non-negotiable sovereign domain. Traditional distribution networks—including corporate app gatekeepers and centralized merchant accounts—demand total compliance with arbitrary guidelines.

✦ SECTION II

The Privacy Equation: Rejecting the Play Store

The Google Play Store policies strictly limit and forbid applications from deploying robust, device-level screenshot and screen-recording blockades, which they claim restrict system-level usability.

For visual content creators who monetize specialized, exclusive high-definition aesthetics (including fashion model lookbooks and face-free foot/leg elegance), screen grabs are a form of financial theft. Sideloading SoleLuxe as an independent standalone package enables the complete integration of **FLAG_SECURE encapsulation**.

This hardware-backed security protocol rendering engine completely blocks screenshot tools, system screen-recorders, and screen-mirror hijack tools. We prioritize your absolute financial safety and physical privacy over standard corporate app store visibility.

✦ SECTION III

Real 80/20 Payout Autonomy

Conventional credit card processors and subscription sites leech upwards of 30% to 50% of your raw income, claiming maintenance and security overheads. Worse, they hold your hard-earned funds in custody for weeks at a time.

By building our transaction layer on-chain via the high-speed **TRON TRC-20 protocol**, we eliminate middleman custody blocks completely. Subscriptions are routed instantly: exactly **80% clears directly into the creator's decentralized wallet**, and **20% settles into the secure platform vault** to power server relays and verification nodes. All mathematics are verifiable publicly on-chain—with zero delays and zero possibility of account seizure.
